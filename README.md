# Vornado Realty Trust

Vornado Realty Trust (NYSE: VNO) is a fully integrated Real Estate Investment Trust (REIT) focused on owning and operating premier office and retail properties in New York City. With nearly 20 million square feet of office space and over 2.4 million square feet of Manhattan street retail, Vornado is among the largest commercial real estate companies in the United States.

**Website:** https://www.vno.com/  
**Type:** Fortune 500 Company  
**Tags:** Commercial Real Estate, REIT, New York City, Fortune 500

---

## About

Vornado Realty Trust is headquartered at 888 Seventh Avenue in New York City and trades on the NYSE under ticker symbol VNO. The company's strategy is concentrated on two core segments:

- **Office:** Nearly 20 million square feet of premier NYC office properties, primarily in the Penn District (Penn Plaza / Pennsylvania Station area) and Manhattan
- **Retail:** Over 2.4 million square feet of Manhattan street retail — the largest owner and manager of street retail in Manhattan

Additional holdings include approximately 2 million square feet of marketplaces, over 2,000 residential units, and advertising signage in Times Square and Pennsylvania Station.

Vornado holds LEED certification for over 27 million square feet of properties and maintains ENERGY STAR recognition as part of its sustainability leadership.
